IB-MYP&DP Introduction

The International Baccalaurate is the international program of successful schools. When a school joins the International Baccalaurate, it officially becomes an international school, meaning that the school gets to join international courses, programs and tournaments. And this improves the school's reputation in real time.

Homework And Materials

IB-MYP&DP students can access their homework and materials through the Toddle platform. Toddle is an online platform where teachers can upload homework, assignments and resources for students to access. Students can log in to their Toddle accounts using their school credentials. IB also gives access to international book publishings and materials like code.org, microbit, My Pals Are Here, Richmond Studios and Oxford University.

Exams And Grading System

IB-MYP&DP students are graded on a scale of 1-8, with 8 being the highest grade. The grading system is based on a combination of internal assessments and external examinations. Internal assessments are graded by the teachers, while external examinations are graded by IB examiners. The final grade is determined by combining the internal assessment and external examination scores.

Benefits of IB-MYP&DP

  • International Recognition: IB-MYP&DP is recognized by universities and employers worldwide, making it easier for students to apply for higher education and job opportunities.
  • Holistic Education: IB-MYP&DP focuses on developing students' intellectual, personal, emotional and social skills, providing a well-rounded education.
  • Critical Thinking: IB-MYP&DP encourages students to think critically and independently, fostering a love for learning and inquiry.
  • Cultural Awareness: IB-MYP&DP promotes cultural understanding and global citizenship, preparing students to thrive in a diverse world.
