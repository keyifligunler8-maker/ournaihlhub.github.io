How can we use parenting techniques to give our children the attention and understand their struggles and problems even though they are small to stop them from disrespecting us? - Parents

We can use the parenting techniques listed below:

  • 1- Visualizing: Visualizing is a technique used by top teachers to increase or activate empathy in narcissistic or rational children. Here is an example on how to apply this technique: If a student wants to write on the board about an important subject about class but nobody in the classroom did because of respect and obiedience training, the teacher says: "Visualize what would your classmates do if you wrote on the board now." then lets student B say: "I never saw anyone write on the board in class and I always wanted to, but I couldn't. Now you activated our desires and we'll want to write too! And the lesson will be ended and we'll not be learning anything which is our biggest fear because there are exams. " And they'll say: Can I write too! and the rational student will understand and go back to her or his seat.
  • 2- Self-Visualizing: If your child cries because her teddy bear was lost, don't say "Stop being dramatic! There's nothing wrong and you're sassy because you're spoiled." Visualize what would you do when you were her age or remind yourself of a similar situation you experienced during childhood but don't beat her but when you understand, say "I understand you, this happened to me too. We can buy a new one with dad's money, my money or with your savings. I know it will never replace your old teddy bear, but the soul of the old teddy bear will live in the new one's." and make your child happy.
  • 3- Expression of Emotions and unseen experiences: If your child needs to be paid attention, listen to his or her reasons and don't judge them, say: "Even if you play on low volume I heard those high volume sounds of e.g violin ......................... at work today and it was really disturbing me. May you do something else or play in another room?" and he or she will listen to you.
  • 4- If none on these work, please take your child and yourself to therapy. If you think therapy is useless because one time you went there and it didn't fix you in another town, don't give up. Get help with a therapist or an intelligent calm school teacher.
